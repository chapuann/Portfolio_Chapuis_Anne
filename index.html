About Me

Hello! I am a science communication and research dissemination specialist focused on climate change and sea-level rise. With 17 years of experience, I design communication strategies, educational resources and decision-support tools that translate complex research into clear, visual and actionable information. I coordinated communication for the EU project PROTECT on future sea-level rise and have led the development of policy briefs, videos, exhibitions, educational tools and the Sea Level Projection Tool.

I'm passionate about making science engaging for all audiences, whether through interactive activities, exhibitions, or educational tools. My goal is to inspire positive action on climate change and raise awareness of the importance of the environment.


2020 - present: Communication science officer, CNRS

2017 - 2020: Teacher, French National Education

2013 - 2017: Climate science communication consultant, Norwegian Environment Agency, Norway

2012 - 2012: Environmental Consultant, Norway

2007 - 2011: PhD in Glaciology, Norway
