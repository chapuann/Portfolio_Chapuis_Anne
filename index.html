About Me

Hello! I'm Anne Chapuis, science communicator and climate education expert with 17 years of experience in developing engaging educational resources and making climate science accessible to all.

I'm passionate about making science engaging for all audiences, whether through interactive activities, exhibitions, or educational tools. My goal is to inspire positive action on climate change and raise awareness of the importance of the environment.


2020 - present: Communication science officer, CNRS

2017 - 2020: Teacher, French National Education

2013 - 2017: Climate science communication consultant, Norwegian Environment Agency, Norway

2012 - 2012: Environmental Consultant, Norway

2007 - 2011: PhD in Glaciology, Norway
